Air-fryer Honey Sesame Salmon Bites

Super easy / healthy-ish preparation of salmon over rice.

February 18, 2026

Makes 2 portions

Modified from a recipe by @kulas_kitchen.

~1 lb. skin-off salmonTrader Joes carries everything you need for this recipe. (Atlantic is less fishy than Alaskan/Pacific if thats what you prefer)

Marinade:
1 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp chili crisp

For serving:
rice
mayo
green onions
sesame seeds
avocado

Steps

  1. Cut salmon into bite-sized chunks (large dice).
  2. Marinate salmon in a mixture of all of the other ingredients for >1 hour.
  3. Cook in an air fryer at 380–400°F for ~10–15 minutes, stirring halfway through.

Can be served with sticky rice, mayo, green onions, sesame seeds, and avocado. We also like to cook some broccolini in sesame oil as a side.
