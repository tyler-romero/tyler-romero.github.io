Recipe Box

I love cooking despite being a very middle of the road home cook. I find it to be relaxing and I like that I get to actually make something in the physical world. I keep an old-school recipe box filled with note cards of recipes that I enjoyed making and that turned out well. So I thought a good way to start this page would be to describe With attribution provided as appropriate. some of those recipes here the next time I decide to make them.

Recipes

  • Chanterelle Pasta with Roasted Garlic Wine Sauce - great recipe for when chantrelles are in season.
  • Greek-Style Lemon Chicken Orzo Soup - a really good soup from Carissa Stanton (@broccyourbody).
  • Pearled Couscous with Shallots and Parsley - slightly modified from a Trader Joe's box.
  • Family Pesto Recipe - simple homemade basil pesto recipe.
  • Air-fryer Honey Sesame Salmon Bites - the easiest way to cook salmon.
  • Sourdough Crumpets - king arthur recipe for using sourdough starter discard.
